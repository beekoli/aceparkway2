About The Project

Noida's Finest Ultra-Luxury Address

CRC The Peridona is an ultra-luxury residential development spread across 6.80 acres inside the iconic Jaypee Greens township. With just 341 exclusive residences across 7 premium towers, this is Noida's rarest low-density luxury project.

Every residence enjoys dual-side views of the golf course and nature park. Rising to 43 floors with a 160-metre tower height, The Peridona redefines what it means to live at the top in Noida.
